# Project Overview

## Purpose

SocialPainFinderAgent turns user-provided comments, reviews, feedback, and exported text into structured pain-point and business-opportunity research. It is a local-first portfolio project that demonstrates how a transparent analysis workflow can connect Voice of Customer data with Marketing, product discovery, AI Agent ideation, automation services, and content strategy.

## Target users

- Marketers and product researchers analyzing qualitative feedback
- Content creators looking for audience pain points and topic ideas
- Students learning business analytics, Marketing, or AI product design
- Course teams reviewing learning barriers and support requests
- Small businesses identifying repetitive workflows and service problems
- Portfolio reviewers evaluating practical Python and AI workflow skills

## Problem it solves

Feedback datasets often contain useful demand signals, but raw comments are difficult to compare. Teams may know that users are unhappy without knowing:

- which problems appear most often;
- which complaints are emotionally intense or urgent;
- which problems imply willingness to pay or business loss;
- which workflows may fit AI automation;
- which themes could become useful content;
- what small MVP should be tested first.

The application organizes these signals into explainable categories, scores, evidence comments, opportunity levels, product hypotheses, and exportable reports.

## Why it fits a portfolio

The project connects several capabilities in one coherent workflow:

- **Marketing:** Voice of Customer analysis, segmentation of complaints, content-topic discovery, and monetization hypotheses.
- **Business analytics:** transparent weighted scoring, prioritization, evidence presentation, and limitations.
- **AI Agent design:** Agent/Skill suggestions, workflow decomposition, human review, MVP scope, and user-test planning.
- **Python engineering:** modular loading, cleaning, rules, scoring, testing, visualization, and local exports.
- **Product communication:** Chinese-first UI, screenshot-ready dashboard, documentation, and standalone reports.

## Main workflow

1. The user uploads CSV, XLSX, or TXT files, or pastes text manually.
2. The loader standardizes comments into a common schema.
3. Cleaning removes empty and duplicate text while preserving original evidence.
4. Bilingual rules detect one or more pain categories.
5. Local rules score emotion and business-opportunity signals.
6. The dashboard ranks opportunities and displays supporting comments.
7. Rule-based templates suggest AI Agents, Skills, workflows, content, services, and validation steps.
8. The user downloads or explicitly saves CSV, Markdown, and HTML results.

## Example outputs

- A ranked table of pain categories and total opportunity scores
- Top 5 opportunity cards with evidence and recommended actions
- Market-signal strength and user-urgency labels
- Monetization angles and suitable scenarios
- AI Agent, AI Skill, and workflow suggestions
- Short-video and Xiaohongshu / Douyin content directions
- Founder / Product Lens with MVP and first-user-test ideas
- UTF-8-sig CSV analysis details
- Chinese-first Markdown and standalone HTML reports

All included sample comments are synthetic. Outputs are directional research signals and require human validation.
